About Antislot

Antislot is a personal recovery companion designed for people who want to take a break from — or fully step away from — gambling. The app provides daily check-ins, urge-logging tools, milestone tracking, mindfulness sessions, and an AI-assisted coaching chat. All progress data is stored on the user's device by default.

Antislot is published by an independent developer and is available on the Apple App Store and Google Play. Premium features are offered as an auto-renewable subscription billed through the user's Apple ID or Google account.

Subscription & billing summary

Antislot Premium is offered as an auto-renewing subscription. The subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends. Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google account. Subscriptions can be managed and canceled in your device account settings at any time.

Full subscription terms — including pricing, renewal cadence, and cancellation rules — are available in the Terms of Use, and step-by-step cancellation / refund instructions live in the Support Center.

Crisis & safety notice

Antislot is a self-help tool. It is not a medical device and does not replace professional advice from qualified clinicians, therapists, or financial advisors.

If you or someone you care about is in crisis or in immediate danger:

  • Türkiye — emergency: dial 112.
  • Türkiye — YEDAM (Yeşilay Danışmanlık Merkezi): dial 115 for free, confidential addiction support.
  • Outside Türkiye: please contact your local emergency number or a trusted health professional.
