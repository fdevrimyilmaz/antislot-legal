Antislot Support

We're here to help.

Get help with your Antislot account, premium subscription, privacy requests, or technical issues. We typically reply within 1 business day (Mon–Fri, 09:00–18:00 GMT+3).

Contact us

The fastest way to reach our support team is by email:

support@antislot.app

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and (if relevant) the email address tied to your Apple ID purchase. The more detail you share, the faster we can resolve your issue.

Subscription & billing

Antislot Premium is offered as an auto-renewing subscription billed through your Apple ID. To view, cancel, or change your plan at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions.
  3. Select Antislot Premium and choose Cancel or change plan.

You can also manage subscriptions directly here: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Refund requests for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Submit a refund request at reportaproblem.apple.com. If you need a written confirmation from us to support your request, email support@antislot.app.

Restore purchases: If you reinstall the app or switch devices, open the Premium screen and tap "Satın Almaları Geri Yükle" (Restore Purchases) while signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Account & data requests

Antislot stores your recovery progress on your device by default. You can request the following at any time by emailing support@antislot.app:

  • Data export — a copy of any data we hold tied to your account.
  • Data deletion — permanent removal of your account and associated data.
  • Privacy questions — anything covered (or not covered) in our Privacy Policy.

We process requests in line with applicable privacy regulations, typically within 30 days.

Troubleshooting

Before contacting support, these quick steps resolve the most common issues:

  • Premium didn't activate after purchase: open the Premium screen and tap Satın Almaları Geri Yükle. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID that completed the purchase.
  • Notifications not arriving: verify Settings → Notifications → Antislot is set to Allow, then restart the app.
  • App is slow or crashes: force-quit and reopen the app; if the issue persists, update to the latest iOS and Antislot version.
  • Can't sign in / data missing: contact us with your Apple ID email and device details so we can investigate.

Crisis & safety

Antislot is a self-help tool and is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or financial advice. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health emergency:

  • Türkiye: dial 112 for emergency services.
  • Türkiye Yeşilay Danışmanlık Merkezi (YEDAM): 115 — confidential addiction support, free of charge.
  • International: contact your local emergency number or a trusted health professional.
