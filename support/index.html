1. Contact us

The fastest way to reach the Antislot support team is by email:

support@antislot.app

To help us resolve your issue on the first reply, please include:

  • A short description of what happened and what you expected to happen.
  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro, iPad Air M3).
  • Your operating system version (e.g., iOS 18.4).
  • Your Antislot app version (visible at the bottom of the in-app Settings screen).
  • If your question is about a purchase: the email address tied to the Apple ID used for the purchase, and the approximate date.
  • Screenshots or a short screen recording, if relevant.

Please do not include passwords, full credit card numbers, or sensitive medical information in your message. We never need these to help you.

2. Subscription & billing help

Antislot Premium is offered as an auto-renewing subscription billed through your Apple ID (iOS) or Google account (Android). Plans renew automatically at the end of each billing period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends.

2.1 View, change, or cancel your subscription

On iPhone or iPad:

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Tap your name at the top of the screen.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select Antislot Premium.
  5. Choose Cancel Subscription, change your plan, or update payment details.

You can also manage subscriptions directly here: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

On Android: open the Google Play Store app → tap your profile picture → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Antislot Premium.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period, and you keep access to premium features until then.

2.2 Refunds

All refunds for App Store purchases are processed directly by Apple, not by Antislot. To request a refund:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID.
  3. Find the Antislot purchase and choose Request a refund.

For Google Play purchases, follow Google's refund instructions.

If you need a written confirmation from us to support your refund request, email support@antislot.app and we'll respond promptly.

2.3 Restore purchases

If you reinstall the app, switch to a new device, or your premium access disappears unexpectedly:

  1. Open Antislot and go to the Premium screen.
  2. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID (or Google account) used for the original purchase.
  3. Tap "Satın Almaları Geri Yükle" (Restore Purchases).

If your subscription does not appear after restoring, contact us with your Apple ID email and the approximate purchase date and we'll investigate.

2.4 Family Sharing

Antislot Premium subscriptions are not currently configured for Family Sharing. Each user must purchase or restore their own subscription.

3. Account & data requests

Antislot does not require you to create an account, and your recovery data is stored on your device by default. You can still request the following at any time, free of charge, by emailing support@antislot.app:

  • Data export — a copy of any data we hold that is associated with your purchase or support history.
  • Data deletion — permanent removal of any server-side records linked to you (your on-device data can be cleared from in-app settings or by uninstalling the app).
  • Correction — correction of inaccurate information.
  • Withdrawal of consent — opt out of any processing based on your consent (e.g., diagnostic reporting).

We process verified requests within 30 days. For full details about your rights, see our Privacy Policy.

4. Troubleshooting

Most issues can be resolved with one of these quick steps. If you still need help, contact us with the details listed in section 1.

4.1 Premium did not activate after purchase

  1. Open the Premium screen and tap Satın Almaları Geri Yükle.
  2. Confirm you are signed in to the same Apple ID that completed the purchase.
  3. Check your internet connection and try again after 1–2 minutes.
  4. If the issue persists, send us your Apple ID email + purchase date.

4.2 Notifications are not arriving

  1. Go to iOS Settings → Notifications → Antislot.
  2. Make sure Allow Notifications is on, and Banner / Sound / Badges are enabled.
  3. Check that Focus / Do Not Disturb is not silencing Antislot.
  4. Force-quit Antislot and reopen it.

4.3 The app is slow, freezes, or crashes

  1. Force-quit and reopen the app.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. Update to the latest version of iOS / Android and the latest version of Antislot from the store.
  4. If you have opted in to diagnostics in app settings, the crash report is automatically sent to us — please still email us with a brief description so we can identify it quickly.

4.4 The AI coaching chat is not responding

  1. Check your internet connection.
  2. Try sending a shorter message.
  3. Wait a few minutes — third-party AI providers occasionally throttle requests.
  4. If the problem persists for more than an hour, please report it.

4.5 Data appears to be missing

Antislot stores recovery progress on your device. Reinstalling the app, restoring a backup from another device, or switching devices without an iCloud / Google backup may result in data loss. If you believe data was lost due to a bug, contact us with as much detail as possible — including your device, OS version, and approximate date the data disappeared.

5. Bug reports & feature requests

Antislot is built by a small team and we read every piece of feedback. To submit a bug report or feature request, email support@antislot.app with the subject line Feedback and include:

  • What you tried to do.
  • What happened instead (for bugs) or what you'd like to see (for features).
  • Why this matters to your recovery — context helps us prioritize.

We can't promise to ship every request, but every message helps shape the roadmap.

7. Crisis & safety resources

Antislot is a self-help tool. It is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, legal, or financial advice. If you or someone you care about is in crisis or in immediate danger:

  • Türkiye — emergency: dial 112.
  • Türkiye — YEDAM (Yeşilay Danışmanlık Merkezi): dial 115 for free, confidential addiction support.
  • International: please contact your local emergency number or a trusted health professional.

For non-urgent emotional support, consider speaking with a licensed therapist or counselor in your area.
