1. Who we are

Antislot ("Antislot", "we", "us", "our") is a self-help mobile application that supports people building healthier habits around gambling urges. The app is published on the Apple App Store and Google Play and is operated by an independent developer based in Türkiye.

For the purposes of the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK No. 6698), the EU and UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / UK GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and other applicable data-protection laws, Antislot is the data controller of the limited information described in this policy.

For all privacy matters, including data-subject requests and complaints, please contact: support@antislot.app.

2. Data we process

2.1 Stored on your device only

The following information is written to your device's secure storage (Keychain via expo-secure-store on iOS, EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android) and never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it or contact us about it:

  • Recovery progress, streak counters, milestone history, and personal goals.
  • Urge logs, mood and craving check-ins, and journal entries you create.
  • Mindfulness session history.
  • App preferences (theme, language, notification settings, opt-in toggles).
  • Local cache of your premium subscription state.

Because this data lives on your device, you can clear it at any time from the in-app settings or by uninstalling the app.

2.2 Processed by our servers

  • AI coaching messages. When you send a message in the in-app AI chat, the most recent messages of that conversation are sent to our backend at api.antislot.app and forwarded to the underlying AI provider (OpenAI or Google Gemini) so a reply can be generated. We do not require your name or contact information to use this feature, and we recommend not including identifying personal information in your messages. Conversations are not used to train AI models.
  • Premium purchase receipts. When you purchase or restore an Antislot Premium subscription, the signed App Store receipt (StoreKit 2 JWS) is sent to our backend so it can be cryptographically verified against Apple's certificate chain. We store only the transaction identifier for duplicate-protection and audit purposes; we do not receive your payment card details, billing address, name, or email — those remain with Apple.
  • Operational request logs. Our servers keep short-lived request logs (timestamp, endpoint, response status, truncated IP address) for up to 30 days for security, abuse prevention, and debugging.

2.3 Optional diagnostics

If you enable diagnostics in the app settings, anonymized crash reports and performance traces are sent to our error-reporting provider (Sentry). These reports include device model, operating system version, app version, and a stack trace. They do not include your journal entries, AI messages, or other personal content. Diagnostics are off by default and can be toggled at any time.

2.4 Information we do not collect

Antislot does not collect: your real name, your phone number, your precise location, your contacts, your photo library, your microphone input, biometric data, advertising identifiers, or any data used for cross-app or cross-site tracking. Antislot does not request the Apple App Tracking Transparency permission.

3. How and why we use your data

We process the categories of data described above only for the purposes and on the legal bases shown below.

  • To provide the app's core features (recovery tracking, urge logging, mindfulness, AI coaching, subscription unlocks). Legal basis: performance of a contract with you (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) and our legitimate interests in delivering the service you requested (Art. 6(1)(f)). KVKK basis: performance of a contract / legitimate interest under Art. 5(2).
  • To validate purchases and protect against fraud or duplicate activations. Legal basis: contractual necessity and legitimate interest in protecting the service.
  • To keep the service secure and reliable (rate-limiting, abuse prevention, debugging). Legal basis: legitimate interest.
  • To respond to your requests (support, data subject requests). Legal basis: compliance with a legal obligation and your consent where applicable.
  • To improve stability and performance (only if you opt in to diagnostics). Legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) / KVKK Art. 5(1)).

We do not use your information for automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you.

4. Sharing & sub-processors

We do not sell or rent your personal data, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We share limited information with the following service providers strictly to operate the app:

  • OpenAI, L.L.C. — to generate AI coaching replies when you use the in-app chat. Data handled under OpenAI's enterprise privacy terms; conversations are not used for model training.
  • Google LLC (Gemini API). — alternative AI provider for the same coaching feature.
  • Apple Inc. — for App Store payments, subscription billing, and StoreKit 2 receipt issuance. All payment data stays with Apple.
  • Google LLC (Google Play Billing). — for in-app payments where the Android app is distributed.
  • Functional Software, Inc. dba Sentry. — only if you opt in to diagnostic reporting.
  • Vercel Inc. — hosts this legal & support website.
  • Our cloud / hosting provider — runs the API endpoints described above.

We may also disclose information when we are required to do so by applicable law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of Antislot, our users, or the public.

5. International data transfers

Some of our sub-processors (OpenAI, Google, Apple, Sentry, Vercel) are located in or process data in the United States and other jurisdictions outside Türkiye, the EEA, and the UK. Where personal data is transferred outside these regions, we rely on the standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards offered by those vendors.

For users in Türkiye: cross-border transfers are made in reliance upon the explicit consent of the data subject and/or the legal grounds provided under KVKK Art. 9, including the data controller's commitments to provide adequate protection.

6. Retention

  • On-device data: retained as long as the app is installed; you can clear it at any time from in-app settings or by uninstalling Antislot.
  • AI chat messages: processed in transit only. We do not persist your conversation history on our servers beyond what is needed to generate a single reply.
  • Purchase / transaction records: retained for the lifetime of the subscription, plus the additional period needed to meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations (typically up to 10 years under Turkish tax law).
  • Operational request logs: typically up to 30 days, then deleted or further anonymized.
  • Diagnostic reports (opt-in): retained per Sentry's default retention (typically 30–90 days).

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erase ("right to be forgotten") your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing, including any processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent (such as opt-in diagnostics).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Türkiye residents have the rights set out in Article 11 of the KVKK; EEA / UK residents have the rights set out in Articles 15–22 of the GDPR / UK GDPR; California residents have the rights set out in the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, the right to delete, the right to correct, and the right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (Antislot does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising).

To exercise any of these rights, email support@antislot.app with the subject line "Privacy Request". We respond to verified requests within 30 days. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the data protection authority in your country of residence — in Türkiye, the Kişisel Verileri Koruma Kurumu (KVKK); in the EEA, your national supervisory authority; in the UK, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

8. AI & automated processing

The in-app coaching chat uses third-party large-language-model providers (OpenAI and/or Google Gemini) to generate replies. The replies are produced automatically and are intended for informational and motivational support only. They are not medical, psychological, legal, or financial advice and must not be relied on as a substitute for advice from a qualified professional.

We do not use AI to make automated decisions about you that produce legal effects or similarly significant effects within the meaning of GDPR Article 22. Conversations sent to these AI providers are not used to train their models.

9. Cookies and tracking technologies

The Antislot mobile app does not use cookies or third-party analytics SDKs for marketing, advertising, or cross-app tracking.

This legal & support website (antislot-legal.vercel.app) is hosted on Vercel and uses only the strictly necessary cookies and log data required to deliver the website itself (e.g., basic request logs and content-delivery cookies set by our hosting provider). We do not run third-party advertising or analytics scripts on this site.

10. Children

Antislot is not directed to children. The app is intended for users who are at least 18 years old (or the age of legal majority in their jurisdiction, whichever is higher). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the user's country).

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@antislot.app and we will delete it promptly.

11. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • TLS 1.2+ for all data in transit between the app, our servers, and our sub-processors.
  • Platform-provided secure key stores (Apple Keychain on iOS, EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android) for on-device data.
  • Cryptographic verification of App Store purchase receipts against Apple's root certificate chain.
  • Strict access controls and least-privilege permissions on our backend infrastructure.
  • Regular review of dependencies and security advisories.

No internet service can guarantee 100% security. We continuously review and improve our security practices, and we will notify affected users without undue delay if a personal-data breach occurs that is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms, as required by applicable law.
